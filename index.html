My Project Idea

My Project idea is a fantasy action mmorpg where all the npcs are individual AI and the world will change dependent on the players. What I mean by this is that players will drop into a fully functioning world run by the npcs. They will be given quests by npcs like in normal mmorpgs but none of it will be scripted by developers. The npcs will generate quests themselves based on their own wants and needs. These quests can go from simple delivery quests to all out wars between kingdoms and empires where the power of the players will determine the outcome.

The motivation for this came from the theory that our world is a simulation run by someone else so I would like to create a new fantasy world simulation but also have it be a playground for us. I think it would be very interesting to see how the world turns out with and without human interference.

The features of the game would be like any standard mmo game with quests and combat and crafting. Players will be able to choose from a variety of combat and non-combat classes. Combat classes are those that specialise in fighting against monster and man. While non-combat classes will specialise in crafting items, farming or trading (becoming a merchant). The non-combat classes will also be able to fight just not as well as the combat classes and the combat classes will be able to engage in trade or farming or crafting but they will not be as good as the noncombat classes. Players will be able to interact with the AI npcs through voice chat and by doing so can build relationships with the npcs and get special quests from them. By completing quests players will be able to earn rewards and reputation with higher reputation giving the player the ability to be recognised by common npcs and even form relationships with important npcs such as lords of cities or kings or other such nobility and thus gain quests from them. Players interactions with npcs can affect the world, for instance by gaining the support of the npc nobility the player may be able to usurp the throne and become king themselves or if a player or group of players is strong enough, they may destroy the npc kingdoms and build their own on top of it. Players will also be able to settle unexplored regions of the map and build up their own villages, towns and cities eventually forming their own kingdoms and empires. Unlike other games of the genre where the player is the chosen hero and all the story and events are scripted by programmers this world will be a living breathing world that changes based on the players interaction. Another feature of the game will be that each continent will start with the same copy of the world state. From there the players will interact with the world changing the world in their continent so the game can be very different for each continent.

For the hardware there will be servers all around the world in every continent. Servers will have to be robust in order to get maximum uptime. For the game itself it will run Unreal engine 4 for the graphics and coding. New AI will have to be developed to make the npcs as realistic as possible so that they can have personalities to be able to build relations with players and other npcs. Through utilising their needs and wants they generate appropriate quests to give to players. It will be like google home or amazon’s alexa except the AI will be asking you to do things for them.

For me it will be very hard to develop this project as I have none of the skills required from using the Unreal engine to coding the AI and setting up the servers for it all to run on. Unreal engine itself requires experience in C++ and python is said to be good for programming AI due to its simplicity. People that have these skills won’t be too hard to find and the servers required for such an mmo already exist. In order to get the people and the hardware though will be very expensive.

If the project is successful, we will have created an mmo like no other where the story will entirely be up to the AI npcs and the quests they hand out to the players. The outcomes of those quests could change the state of the world. By doing this Players will create their own stories from their own actions. It will be interesting to see how each of the copies in the different continents will end up and it will also be interesting to see what would happen in a world with no player interaction.
